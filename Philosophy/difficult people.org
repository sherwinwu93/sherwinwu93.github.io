* 13 Steps to Get Along with Difficult People
[[https://www.happierhuman.com/difficult-people/]]
- 1.Be a good listner, especially before reacting.
- 2.Dont be judgmental
- 3.Get someone else's perspective while also avoid gossip.
- 4.Be respectful. Two wrongs don't make a right.
- 5.Give a peace offering.
- 6.Identify their hidden need.
- 7.Offer compliments.
- 8.Don't demand change,but explain how their actions make you feel.
- 9.Try not to take their behavior personally. Know what triggers you about their behavior.
- 10.Find someone who may be able to offer help.
- 11.Self-reflection - Why does their behavior bother you so much?
- 12.Take a good look in the mirror; you may be the difficult one everyone has an issue with.
- 13.Distance yourself. Limit interaction.
* 12 ways to deal with someone who doesn't respect you
[[https://hackspirit.com/ways-to-deal-with-someone-doesnt-respect-you/]]
1. Try not to take it personally.
2. Think before you say something to them
3. Get advice specific to your situation
4. Ask yourself if it's even worth it to respond
5. Confront them about it
6. Understand where they're coming from
7. Set boundaries with them
8. Respond with kindness
9. Ask others for help
10. Walk away from the situation
11. Don't try to change them
12. Avoid being with people that don't respect you
