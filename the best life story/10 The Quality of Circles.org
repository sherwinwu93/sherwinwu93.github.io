* 10 The Quality of Circles
#+begin_verse
Sitting on her mother's lap, reading The Book of Shapes, my daughter came to the triangle page.
'What's this shape?' her mom asked.
Wheels of cogitation began to spin (signified by a mouth scrunched to one side) and after a brief pause (and with the certainty of a jury foreman) she said, 'A circle.'
Mom asked her, 'Are you sure?' (As she did whenever an answer was incorrect.)
But my daughter, sensing that something was amiss, said, 'Yep,' and then pausing, added, 'but it's not a very good one.'
#+end_verse
