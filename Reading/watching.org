1. Watch with Purpose: Before starting the video, have a clear idea of what information you want to learn from it.
2. Use Subtitles: Turn on subtitles if available. This can help you understand spoken English better.
3. Repeat Key Phrases: If you hear a word or phrase you don't understand, pause the video and repeat it. Look it up if needed.
4. Note Important Points: Jot down key information as you hear it. Focus on the main ideas rather than trying to capture everything.
5. Review and Reflect: After watching, review your notes and reflect on what you learned. Try to use the new information in a sentence or two.
6. Repeat as Needed: If you didn't understand everything, don't worry! Rewatch the parts you found challenging until you grasp the main points.
