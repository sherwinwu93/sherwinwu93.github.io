* Lesson 04
** words
have a good trip
1. I have had a great trip in Shanghai last summer.
2. I haven't had a great trip in Shanghai last summer.
3. Have you had a great trip in Shanghai last summer.
4. Why have you had a great trip in Shanghai last summer?
5. When you have had a great trip in Shanghai?
receive sth. from sb.
1. I have received a message from my father.
2. Have you received a message from your father?
3. What have you received from your father?
4. Where have you received a letter from your father?
5. Why have you received a letter from your father?
work for
1. I'm working for Miss Qiu.
2. Who are you working for?
3. Why are you working for Miss Qiu?
4. Are you working for Miss Qiu?
5. Where are you working for Miss Qiu?
A be different from B
1. I was different from my wife.
2. Were you different from your wife?
3. Where were you different from your wife?
4. Why were you different from your wife?
5. Who were you different from?
in the centre of sw.
1. He talking loudly in the centre of the theater.
2. Was he talking loudly in the centre of the theater?
3. Why was he talking loudly in the centre of the theater?
4. Who was talking loudly in the centre of the theater?
5. What was he talking about loudly in the centre of the theater?
go/live/study abroad
1. Miss Feng has already gone abroad.
2. Has Miss Feng gone abroad?
3. Why has Miss Feng gone abroad?
4. Where has Miss Feng gone abroad?
5. How long has Miss Feng gone abroad?
