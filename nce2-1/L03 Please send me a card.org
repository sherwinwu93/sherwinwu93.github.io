* Lesson 3 Please send me a card.
#+begin_verse
How many cards did the writer send?

Postcards always spoil my holidays.
Last summer, I went to Italy.
I visited museums and sat in public gardens.
A friendly waiter *taught me a few words of Italian*.
Then he *lent me a book*.
I read a few lines, but I did not understand a word.
Every day I *thought about* postcards.
My holidays passed quickly, but I did not send cards to my friends.
*On the last day* I made a big decision.
I got up early and bought thirty-seven cards.
I *spent the whole day in my room*, but I did not write a single card!
#+end_verse

** 单词造句
buy sb. sth.
1. I bought my wife an expensive iphone 13.
2. Did you buy your wife an expensive iphone 13?
3. What did you buy for your wife?
4. Why did you buy an expensive iphone 13 for your wife?
5. When did you buy your wife an expensive iphone 13.
send sb. sth.
1. I sent my wife the tea.
2. Did you send your wife tea?
3. Why did you send your wife tea?
4. When did you send tea to your wife?
5. How did you send tea to your brother?
lend sb. sth.
1. I usually lend money to my friend!
2. Did you lend money to your friend?
3. Why did you lend money to your friend?
4. What did you lend to your friend?
5. Where did you lend money to your friend?
borrow sth. from sb.
1. I borrow a book from public library yesterday.
2. What did you borrow from public library yesterday?
3. When did you borrow a book from library?
4. Did you borrow a book from public library yesterday?
5. How often do you borrow books from public library.
make a decision
1. According to the fact, I made this decision.
2. Did you make a decision about learning?
3. What did you make?
4. Why did you make this decision?
5. When did you make this decision?

** 习惯用法造句
teach sb. sth.
1. Sedgewick taught me the knowledge of algorithms.
2. Who taught you the knowledge of algorithms?
3. Did Sedgewick teach you the knowledge of algorithms?
4. Why did Sedgewick teach you the knowledge of algorithms?
think about sth.
1. I think about it every night and every day.
2. What do you think about every night and every day?
3. How often do you think about it?
4. Why do you think about it?
on the last day
1. I had finished my homework on the last day.
2. Did you have finished your homework on the last day?
3. When have you finished your homework?
4. When did you finish your homework on the last day?
spend st. sw.
1. I spend the half day at the office.
2. How long did we spend at the office?
3. Where did you spend the whole day?
4. Why did you spend the half day at the office?

** Ask me if 写+读
1. I went to Italy for my summer holidays. Where/Why/When
	 Where did you go for your summer holidays?
	 Why did you go to Italy for your summer holidays?
	 When did you go to Italy?
2. I visited museums. What/When
	 What did you visit?
	 When did you visit museums?
3. I sat in public gardens. Where
	 Where did you sit?
4. I learnt a little Italian. How much
	 How much Italian did you learn?
5. A friendly waiter taught me. Who
	 Who taught you?
6. He read a book. What/Who
	 What did he read?
	 Who read a book?
7. I read a few lines. How many
	 How many lines did you read?
8. I thought about postcards every day. How often/ What
	 How often did you think about postcards?
	 What did you think everyday?
