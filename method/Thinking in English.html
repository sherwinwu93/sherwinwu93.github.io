1.3. linguamarina

So, thinking in English is a key thing for you to move from being intermediate to upper-intermediate, advanced and native speaker level.

I went to bed thinking in English, and I had a dream in English, and I woke up in the morning, and I realized I'm actually thinking in English.

rules for keeping thinking in English:

  1. travel to an English-speaking country.
  2. From now on I will only do fun stuff in English. YouTube video, Instagram, English books.
  3. learn the concept in English straightaway
  4. When you're learning a new concept you get rid of translation.
    1. if you want to read more about photography or if you want to read more about filmmaking, blogging, do that in English.
    2. if you encounter a new concept, you just Google it, you go to images, and you read what that word means without translating it.
  5. Switch your phone, your smartphone, your laptop, to English.
